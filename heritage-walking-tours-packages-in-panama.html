Frequently Asked Questions About Heritage Walking Tours Packages

What is included in the heritage walking tour packages offered by Hotel Plaza Herrera / THErestaurant?
Our heritage walking tour packages provide a guided exploration of Casco Viejo, Panama City's UNESCO-listed historic district. Each package includes a professional, bilingual guide, entrance fees to select heritage sites, complimentary bottled water, and a curated route highlighting architectural landmarks, local culture, and hidden gems. For guests booking through the hotel or THErestaurant, we also offer special add-ons like a post-tour tasting menu or drinks at THErestaurant.
How much do the heritage walking tours cost, and are there any discounts for groups or hotel guests?
Our standard walking tour packages start at $35 per person for a 2-hour guided experience. We offer a 10% discount for hotel guests and groups of 5 or more. Children under 12 join for half price. Customized private tours or tours including exclusive site access may have different pricing—please contact our concierge for a detailed quote.
Do I need to book the tour in advance, and how long does the tour usually last?
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance as tours often fill up, especially on weekends and local holidays. Most heritage tours last between 2 to 2.5 hours, covering key historical sites at a comfortable walking pace. Last-minute bookings may be accommodated depending on guide availability—please inquire at our front desk or through THErestaurant.
Are your tour guides licensed, and do they speak languages other than Spanish?
Yes, all our guides are officially licensed by the Panama Tourism Authority and have extensive experience with Casco Viejo's heritage sites. Tours are conducted in both English and Spanish; if you require another language, please let us know in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.
What happens if it rains or if I need to cancel my tour reservation?
Panama's weather can be unpredictable, but our tours run rain or shine—bring an umbrella or poncho if rain is forecast. If severe weather makes the tour unsafe, we will reschedule or provide a full refund. For cancellations, please notify us at least 12 hours before your scheduled tour for a full refund; late cancellations may incur a fee. Hotel guests can reschedule at no charge, subject to availability.
